About Me...

Charles Modi on the right

My name is Charles Modi, I was born on the 1st January 1999 and I am currently in my first year at Manchester Metropolitan Studying Computer Science.


At Holy Cross College I studied Computer Science, History and Biology, these three subjects hardly interlink with each other. During A Levels, I discovered that I preferred Computer Science compared to the other two and I excelled at it, I chose to continue my studies at MMU. I enjoyed the independence during A Level Computer Science while working on my project, I was allowed to go at my own pace and I gained skills such as excellent time management.

Professionalism

Teamwork and Leadership

I am comfortable working as part of a group, doing my part to contribute and at times doing tasks that others may be unwilling to do to ensure the team’s success. I generally consider myself as a follower compared to a leader, I like to get involved in the task set.

However, when put in a group where people are unorganised and uncertain of the task set, I am willing to take leadership and organising the group to ensure that the task is complete within the set deadline.

Currently in Information systems we are working in groups on a project which requires the entire groups involvement, we held meetings and discussed people's roles, checked on the progress of everyone and overall making sure that the work is submitted in on time.

In the group I was one of the two who made sure that everything was organised and ran smoothly by gathering the group together and setting people tasks. The group listened to each other’s’ ideas and made sure that everyone contributed.
